Gain Weight

  1. Add healthy calories. You don’t need to drastically change your diet.
  2. Go nutrient dense. Instead of eating empty calories and junk food, eat foods that are rich in nutrients.
  3. Snack away. Enjoy snacks that contain plenty of protein and healthy carbohydrates
  4. Eat mini meals. If you’re struggling with a poor appetite, due to medical or emotional issues, eating large amounts of food may not seem appealing.
  5. Bulk up. While too much aerobic exercise will burn calories and work against your weight goal, strength training can help

Danger!You Need To Lots of Weight

  1. Exercise regularly. You need to get 150 to 300 minutes of moderate-intensity activity a week to prevent weight gain
  2. Follow a healthy-eating plan. Focus on low-calorie, nutrient-dense foods, such as fruits, vegetables and whole grains.
  3. Know and avoid the food traps that cause you to eat. Identify situations that trigger out-of-control eating.
  4. Monitor your weight regularly. People who weigh themselves at least once a week are more successful in keeping off excess pounds.
  5. Be consistent. Sticking to your healthy-weight plan during the week, on the weekends, and amidst vacation
